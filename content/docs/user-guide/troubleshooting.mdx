## Quick Connection Issues

### Can't Connect to qBittorrent

**Symptoms**: "Connection failed" or "Unable to connect"

**Quick Solutions**:
1. **Verify qBittorrent is running** with Web UI enabled
2. **Check connection details** (host: localhost, port: 8080)
3. **Test direct access** to `http://localhost:8080`

For detailed troubleshooting steps, see the [main troubleshooting guide](../troubleshooting/index.mdx).
