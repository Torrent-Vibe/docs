### Pricing Questions

**Is Torrent Vibe really free to try?**
Yes! Download the trial version and use all features without any restrictions or time limits.

**What does "buy once, own forever" mean?**
You pay $9.99 one time and get permanent access to the software. No subscriptions, no recurring fees.

**Are there any hidden costs?**
No hidden costs. The $9.99 covers everything: software, updates within the major version, source code, and support.

**Do I get updates after purchasing?**
Yes, you get all updates within the current major version for free. Major version upgrades are separate purchases.

### Purchase Questions

**How do I get my license key?**
After purchase, you'll receive an email with your license key instantly. The email also includes activation instructions.

**Can I use the license on multiple devices?**
Yes, you can use your license on multiple devices as long as you're the primary user.

**What if I lose my license key?**
Contact our support team and we'll resend your license key to your registered email address.

**Is the purchase instant?**
Yes, digital delivery is instant. You'll receive your license key immediately after payment confirmation.

### Technical Questions

**Do I need qBittorrent installed?**
Yes, Torrent Vibe is a client interface for qBittorrent. You need qBittorrent running separately.

**Which qBittorrent versions are supported?**
Torrent Vibe supports qBittorrent API v2, compatible with qBittorrent 4.1.0 and later versions.

**Can I get a refund?**
Due to the unlimited trial and digital nature of the software, we don't offer refunds. Please try the trial thoroughly first.

**Is my data safe?**
Absolutely. Torrent Vibe runs locally and never stores your data on external servers. It only connects to your local qBittorrent instance.

### License Questions

**Can I use Torrent Vibe commercially?**
Yes, the lifetime license includes commercial use rights.

**Do licenses expire?**
No, lifetime licenses never expire. You own the software permanently.

**Can I transfer my license?**
Licenses are non-transferable. Each user needs their own license.

**What happens with major version upgrades?**
Major version upgrades (like 1.x to 2.x) are separate purchases, but you get all 1.y updates for free.
