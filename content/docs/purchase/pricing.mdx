## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Can I upgrade my license?**
Yes, you can upgrade at any time. The price difference will be prorated.

**Is there a free trial?**
Yes, all paid plans include a 30-day free trial.

**Can I use Torrent Vibe commercially?**
Commercial use requires a Personal license or higher.

**Do you offer refunds?**
Yes, 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases.

### Technical Questions

**How is the license delivered?**
License keys are delivered instantly via email after purchase.

**Can I use the license on multiple devices?**
Yes, licenses can be used on multiple devices for the same user.

**What happens if I lose my license key?**
Contact support - we can resend your license key.

**Do licenses expire?**
Subscription licenses renew annually. Lifetime licenses never expire.

### Billing Questions

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Credit cards, PayPal, bank transfer, and purchase orders.

**Can I change my billing cycle?**
Yes, you can switch between monthly and annual billing.

**Do you offer discounts for multiple licenses?**
Yes, volume discounts are available for 5+ licenses.

**Is VAT/GST included?**
Taxes are added at checkout based on your location.
