## Information We Collect

### No Personal Data Collection
Torrent Vibe does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The application operates entirely on your local device without any data collection servers.

### License Verification Only
- **Creem Service**: We use Creem's third-party license verification service
- **Minimal Data**: Only license keys are transmitted for validation purposes
- **No Storage**: Verified licenses are not stored on external servers
- **Local Processing**: License validation occurs locally when possible

## How We Use Information

### License Management
- License keys are used solely for product activation and validation
- No license data is retained beyond the verification process
- Verification results are stored locally on your device only

### No Data Sharing
- We do not share any information with third parties
- No analytics, tracking, or usage data is collected
- No advertising or marketing data collection

## Third-Party Services

### Creem License Verification

**Information Provided to Creem**:
- Email address and name (for account creation and verification)
- Credit card information (processed securely by Creem for payment)
- Subject to Creem's privacy policy at [creem.io/privacy](https://creem.io/privacy)

**Information Provided to Us**:
- License key (for activation and validation)
- Email address (for verification and support)
- GitHub username (for repository access invitation)

**Purpose**: License verification, repository access, customer support, and update notifications.

**Consequences of Not Providing Information**:
- **To Creem**: You will be unable to complete purchases
- **To Us**: You may not receive timely software updates or source code access

**Data Storage**: We may store your email address, license activation code, and GitHub account name for license validation, repository access management, customer support, and update notifications.

## User Rights

### Data Control
- You maintain full control over all your data
- No data collection means no data to delete or export
- Uninstalling the application removes all associated data

### Transparency
- This privacy policy is comprehensive and complete
- No hidden data collection practices
- Clear explanation of all data handling

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in our documentation and application updates.

## Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please use our support channels as outlined in our documentation.
